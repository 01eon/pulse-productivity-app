Pulse is more than a task manager—it’s your personal productivity rhythm coach. The app intelligently adapts to your energy levels, offering dynamic scheduling, focus timers, and smart break reminders to help you stay in sync with your optimal working patterns. By combining actionable insights, intuitive tools, and a sleek design, Pulse transforms the way you approach your day, ensuring not just productivity but also balance and well-being.
